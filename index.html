Privacy Policy for Twino Bible

Last updated: April 2026

Twino Bible does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, and does not display advertisements.

Content Downloads

All content (Bible translations, devotions, song books) is downloaded directly to your device and stored locally. No personal data is transmitted to any server beyond downloading content files from our public GitHub repository.

Local Storage

Notification preferences, reading settings, bookmarks, highlights and notes are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted anywhere.

Notifications

If you enable the daily verse reminder, the notification is scheduled locally on your device. No data leaves your device for this purpose.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: your@email.com

Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
